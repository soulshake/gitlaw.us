The idea is out there.

I don't know what it would look like, or how it would get adopted. Do we start with a handful of Congresspeople, or on a state level, or with a legion of volunteers maintaining it in parallel until it gets adopted? I don't know, but whatever it is, it has to bridge the current and widening divide between the lawmakers and the citizens subjected to those laws.

Pretty please?
